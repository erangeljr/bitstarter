Vitally Mine will be a place where you can centrally manage your and your families import health information. Many of us have seen more than one physician in our life. When it comes to your health information it is crucial that you have your health information when you need. Not locked up in some medical providers offices. At Vitally Mine, we will provide you a mechanism to store and retrieve your health information when you want it.

At Vitally Mine, we know how important it is to have your health information available to you when you need it. That's why we have developed the Vitally Mine Web Applicaiton and Web Service. This will allow you to store and retreive you health information from our Web Application or your favorite mobile device.

FAQ

What type of information can I store?
Initially, you will be able to store all of your vital signs. i.e. Blood Preasure, Blood Type, Height, and Weight
Will my information be secure?
Yes, given the sensitiveness of your personal health information, we ensure that we follow all the HIPPA and HiTech guideliens established.
